Previous Speakers

Dr. Don Vaughn

Neuroscientist

Neuroscientist, science communicator, behavioural data consultant, DJ and a model. His research has been featured in TIME magazine. A TED speaker. His TED talk “Neurohacking: rewiring your brain” received over a million views. An adjunct faculty at Santa Clara University.

Abhas Mitra

Astrophysicist

An Indian astrophysicist. Best known for his distinct views on several astrophysics concepts, particularly related to black hole and Big Bang theory involving the black hole information paradox. He resolved the black hole information paradox He resolved the black hole information paradox 13 years before Stephen Hawking did and it is believed that he did it in a way that is superior. A member of the Indian Astronomical Union.TED speaker. Among the top 3 most talked about astrophysicists in the world.

Anima Patil-Sabale

Scientist-Astronaut

Anima Patil-Sabale is a Scientist-Astronaut Candidate for commercial space projects PoSSUM and PHEnOM and she aspires to be a NASA Astronaut. She's a Software & Aerospace Engineer, she has worked on NASA's Kepler Mission and in NASA's Intelligent Systems Division. Anima is also a pilot and a certified scuba diver. Anima has been a Commander for NASA's HERA VII, a simulated asteroid mission.

Kailash Srivastava

Power System Professional

Vice Chancellor at Symbiosis University of Applied Science, Indore. He is a power system professional with well-rounded experience across various sectors across globe. He has been working for ABB Global Research lab in Sweden for last 18 years. He offers academic services as an adjunct professor to Lulea Technical University in Sweden and IIT Kanpur. He is a TED speaker.

Dr. Charles H. Lineweaver

Associate Professor

Dr Charles H. Lineweaver is the convener of the Australian National University's Planetary Science Institute and holds a joint appointment as an associate professor in the Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics and the Research School of Earth Sciences.

Kulpreet Yadav

Author, Motivational Speaker

Kulpreet Yadav is a bestselling author, motivational speaker, start-up mentor and Founder-Editor of Open Road Review, South Asia's leading online magazine of literature and culture. Shortlisted in various writing contests, his short stories and essays have appeared in over thirty publications.

Dr. V.N Mani

Scientist

Dr V. N. Mani completed his (B.Sc.-Physics) in 1983 and (M.Sc., Physics-Electronics, Presidency College, University of Madras) in 1985 and Ph.D. (III-V epitaxial Semiconductors Modelling areas-Anna University-Technical, Chennai) in 1990.

Sarang Verma

CEO, Appointy Software Inc.

Sarang Verma is the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Bhopal based start-up APPOINTY. Appointy was launched in 2007 by Nemesh along with his four friends including Sarang Verma. It is an online scheduling firm which now has customers in more than 110 countries.

Dr. H.C Verma

Author, Concepts of Physics

Harish Chandra Verma is an Indian experimental physicist (Scientist) and professor in the Department of Physics at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. All engineering aspirants best recognize him as the author of their bible-Concepts of Physics.

JOHN C. MATHER

Senior Astrophysicist

Dr. John C. Mather is a Senior Astrophysicist in the Observational Cosmology Laboratory at NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center. He is a Nobel Prize in Physics (2006) laureate for his work on the Cosmic Background Explorer Satellite (COBE) with George Smoot.

Dr. A.S Pillai

Scientist

Dr. Pillai graduated in Electrical Engineering from Madras University and underwent Management Program at Harvard Business School. Dr. Pillai’s four decades of experience in ISRO and DRDO under the great visionary leaders Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, Prof. Satish Dhawan and Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam.

SACHIN GUPTA

Co-Founder, HackerEarth

Sachin Gupta is an IIT Roorkee grad, ex – Googler and now heads Sales/Marketing/Operations at HackerEarth. He interned as well as worked with the likes of Microsoft (2011) and Google (2012) and always stayed high on code. Taking this passion forward, he and his friend, Vivek Prakash.

Dr. Viswanathan Raghunathan

CEO, Academic, Author, Columnist

Dr. V. Raghunathan (born 1954) is an academic, author, columnist, hobbyist and a CEO. He is currently, Director, Schulich School of Business (York University, Toronto), India (Hyderabad Campus). Since 2013, for three consecutive years, Dr. Raghunathan has been featured in the list of top 50 thinkers.

Walter Lewin

Former Professor, MIT

Walter Hendrik Gustav Lewin is a Dutch astrophysicist and former professor of physics at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Lewin earned his doctorate in nuclear physics in 1965 at the Delft University of Technology and was a member of MIT's physics faculty for 43 years beginning in 1966.

Senior Technologist, Texas Instruments India

Chief - Strategy, Architecture & Engineering at Bharti Airtel (India)
