About

SCEECS

IEEE International Students' Conference on Electrical, Electronics and Computer Sciences(SCEECS) is being organized by the institution in the City of Lakes, MANIT Bhopal in February 2022 by IEEE MANIT student branch. This flagship conference of IEEE M.P Sub Section will feature a comprehensive technical program including paper presentations, workshops and expert guest lectures.

SCEECS Past Events

SCEECS'14

Students´ Conference on Electrical, Electronics and Computer Science (SCEECS 2014) was organised on March 1 and 2, 2014 which involved paper and poster presentation by U.G. and P.G. scholars.

SCEECS'16

IEEE Student Branch of MANIT organised IEEE Students’ Conference on Electrical, Electronics and Computer Science on 5th-6th March,2016. The conference was inaugurated by Dr. Appu Kuttan KK, Director of MANIT.

SCEECS'18

IEEE MSB had organised various workshops on 24th and 25th February 2018 during its 4th IEEE International Conference SCEECS'18.
